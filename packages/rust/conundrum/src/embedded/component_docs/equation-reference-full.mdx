## Quote
> Note the blockquote

### Example


```jsx
<Quote source="Oscar Wilde" >
There are two great trajedies in life. One is getting what you want, the other is not getting it.
</Quote>
```

Or because this component accepts `Sizable` props, we can do something like:

```jsx
<Quote source="Nag Hamadi Codex" centerContent centerSelf width="medium">
I have cast fire upon the world, and see, I am guarding it until it **blazes**
</Quote>
```

There also exists a `sourceId` property that applications can take advantage of to group your quotes throughout your notes, so use that how you like.
