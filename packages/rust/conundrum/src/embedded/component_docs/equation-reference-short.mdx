## Equation Reference

Reference your equations by their index number without worrying about the structure of your notes changing. Just add the unique `{#myId}` syntax (same as with heading id's) and you'll be able to reference that equation by index anywhere within your note.

### Example

````mdx
This is my awesome equation

$$ {#myEquationsId}
e=mc^2
$$

My awesome equation is numbered <EqRef id="myEquationsId" />!
````
