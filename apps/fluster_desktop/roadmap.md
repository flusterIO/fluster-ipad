## Roadmap

- [ ] Pdf support integrated with the citation manager.
- [ ] Embeddable images & videos integrated with Apple's photos support
  - [ ] Integrate Apple's support for object capture to support embedding 3d models directly in your notes.
- [ ] Support for embedding references to other common features like Events from your calendar, contacts from your on-device contacts list, and other first-party Apple services.
- [ ] Integrate interactive tables powered by Polars.


## Version 2 & beyond

- [ ] Eventually move to a more Jupyter, cell based rendering method that will allow handling more content with Swift where there is benefit (plots, video, etc.).
